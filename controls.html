Arrow Keys
Mirrors the keyboard arrow key layout.
Up floats above, flanked by Left/Down/Right on the bottom row.

Split Row
Left thumb: Left/Down. Right thumb: Up/Right.
Wide gap separates the two sides into distinct thumb zones.
–
Thumbs
Left thumb: Up/Down (vertical). Right thumb: Left/Right (horizontal).
Each axis mapped to the thumb that controls it naturally.
–
Thumbs Lefty
Mirrored for left-handed players. Left thumb: Left/Right. Right thumb: Up/Down.

Virtual Stick
Drag the knob in any cardinal direction.
Snaps to 4 directions — no precise tap target required.
